Acting on Mental Health: A Data-driven Call

For over four decades Americares has pursued research into mental health and socioeconomic data in order to better understand and improve the healthcare and economic lives of North Carolinians. Americares found and concluded that there are significant connections between mental health and socioeconomic factors for the vast amount of North Carolinians. Similarly, Reno (NV), has experienced similar obstacles in their community and have deployed a mental health support application at no cost to their citizens.

This application, Talkspace-Reno, has proven to benefit citizens with little or no healthcare coverage by providing them immediate healthcare access at no cost. Americares is hopeful that with this data-supported presentation the State of North Carolina will aggressively pursue this project in order to better improve the lives of its citizens.
